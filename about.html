My Roots

Home is more than just a place; it is about traditions, culture, religion, and family that build our identity. Growing up in Arequipa, Peru, I was surrounded by a rich culture that values resilience, hard work, community, and devotion. From an early age, I was immersed in traditions that shaped my identity, whether dancing typical dances during the anniversary of my city or attending the procession of the "Virgin of Chapi." The sense of community and delicious food is what I remember most. My roots are not just part of my past but a constant source of strength that reminds me where I come from and the kind of person I want to be.
